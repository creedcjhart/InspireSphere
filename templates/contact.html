Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a review?

To submit a review, you need to create an account and log in. Once logged in, you can click on the "Write Review" button on any product or service page.

How long does it take for my review to be published?

Reviews typically go through a moderation process that takes 24-48 hours to ensure quality and adherence to our guidelines.

Can I edit my review after posting?

Yes, you can edit your review within 7 days of posting. After that, please contact our support team for any necessary changes.
